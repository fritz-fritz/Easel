# Easel name and branding

The Mozilla Public License 2.0 licenses the source code, not project names, logos, store listings,
signing identities, or other branding.

You may make truthful, nominative references to Easel—for example, to say that a fork is based on
Easel. Modified or independently distributed builds must not imply that they are official Easel
packages or are endorsed by the Easel maintainers. Use a distinct product name and visual identity
when needed to avoid confusion.

Official packages are identified by their designated storefront publisher, signing identity, and
update channel. No project logo is included in this repository at this time.
